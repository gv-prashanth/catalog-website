Our Story

Vader and Rincey, now retired veterans once roamed the World of Warcraft always staying together and taking care of each other. It all started in Stranglethorn Vale, 2006 where the naive Rincey was struggling to fight the goblins and the short pro gnomish Vader comes to rescue. From questing to roaming to fighting Horde to Arenas and Battlegrounds they grow more and more strong together. The Journey has led them to one of the strongest 2 player team in both worlds.

VADRIN is a website to showcase and preserve all the projects we worked and will be working together.
